2020-11-16

China‘s annual Singles Day online shopping bonanza regularly hauls in tens of billions of dollars for Alibaba and other e-commerce and retail companies in China.

This year ，it’s taking on new meaning as a showcase for the country’s success in battling the Covid-19 pandemic.

The event already appears to bo on pace to break records again：Alibaba said early Wednesday morning that the annual sales frenzy has so far pulled in 372.3 billion($56.3 billion).

▍语言点

  1. China’s annual Singles Day 一年一度的中国光棍节，双十一

· annual /ˈænjuəl/ adj. 每年的，年度的

  1. online shopping bonanza 网购狂潮

· bonanza /bəˈnænzə/ n. 本意指海上的晴好天气或繁荣；引申为可以赚大钱的时机，发财的机会

  1. haul in 大赚一笔(to earn a lot of money)

  2. e-commerce 电子商务，本节指电商平台

  1. take on 拥有某种特征(have, acquire)

· His life has taken on a new dimension.

他的生活焕然一新。

  1. showcase /ˈʃoʊkeɪs/ n. 玻璃陈列柜；展示本领的场合

· The platform is a good showcase for the talents.

这个平台对于优秀人才来说，是一个很好的展示机会。

  1. battle/fight the Covid-19 pandemic 抗击新冠疫情
  1. be on pace to do sth. 在……节奏上；有望、即将要做某事

· pace /peɪs/ n. 步伐，节奏

  1. break record 破纪录

· be on pace to break record(s) 某事有望破纪录

  1. frenzy /ˈfrenzi/ n. 狂热(bonanza)

  2. pull in大赚一笔(haul in)

  3. regularly 有规律的；经常；均匀地，匀称地；

  4. retail 零售（n）;以…价格销售（v）；详说，述说（尤指别人的事情）(v)

  5. pandemic (全国或全球性的)流行病(n)；大流行的，普遍的，全世界的(adj)

  6. so far 如此程度，这个地步；迄今为止；到目前为止

  7. appear 显得；看来；似乎；出现；呈现；起源；首次使用
