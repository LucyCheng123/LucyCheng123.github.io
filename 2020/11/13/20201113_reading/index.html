2020-11-13

Boris Johnson says he has spoken to the president-elect Joe Biden and congratulated him on his election victory.

The prime minister said in a tweet: “i look forward to strengthening our partnership between our countries and to woking with him on our shared priorities-from tackling climate change, to promoting democracy and building back better from the pandemic.”

The phone call , among the president elect’s first with world leaders ,took place at around 4pm and lasted approximately 25 minutes.

1.president-elect (目前已经当选但尚未正式上任的) 候任总统

president 总统

elect 当选而尚未就任的，候任的

prime minister-elect 候任首相

  1. congratulate /kənˈɡrætʃəleɪt/ v. 祝贺

· congratulate sb. on sth. 祝贺某人某事

· congratulation n. 祝贺

· I’d like to congratulate you on your new job.

=I’d like to give congratulations to you on your new job.

祝贺你找到新工作。

  1. look forward to 期待，盼望着

  2. work with sb. 和某人一道……

  3. shared /ʃerd/ adj. 共享的，共同的

· shared values 共同价值观

· shared rooms 合租房间

· shared interest 共同利益

  1. build back better 灾后重建美好家园

· 这个短语最早出现在2015年第三届联合国世界减灾大会通过的《2015-2030年仙台减少灾害风险框架》文件中。

8:tackle 应付，处理，解决(难题或局面)

9:climate 气候；气候区；倾向；思潮；风气；环境气氛；

10:promote 促进；推动；促销；推销；提升；晋升

11:democracy 民主政体；民主制度；民主国家；民主精神；民主权利；民主

12:approximately 大概；大约；约莫
